The Ballard of Shrek the Slayer

In the land of Far Far Away, where fairy tales come to play,
Lived a mighty ogre, Shrek the Slayer, bold and brave each day.

With a roar that shook the trees and a heart as pure as gold,
He defended the weak, his legend forever told.

Through forests dark and dungeons deep, he fought with all his might,
A hero in the shadows, bringing justice to the night.

So raise your voices high, let the bards proclaim,
The Ballard of Shrek the Slayer, forever in our name.

The Ballard of Shrek the Slayer's Donkey

In the land of Far Far Away, where tales of old are spun,
There lived a talking donkey, whose adventures had just begun.

With a heart full of courage and a spirit wild and free,
He stood by Shrek's side, as loyal as could be.

Through trials and tribulations, their bond grew ever strong,
A friendship forged in fire, where they both belonged.

So let the minstrels sing, let the stories be told,
The Ballard of Shrek's Donkey, a legend to behold.

The Ballard of Shrek the Slayer's Wife,
Fiona the Princess

In the kingdom of Far Far Away, where magic fills the air,
Lived a princess named Fiona, with beauty beyond compare.

But beneath her royal guise, a secret she did keep,
A curse that turned her into an ogre when the night did creep.

Yet love transcended all, as Shrek came into her life,
Together they defied the odds, husband and wife.

So let the tales be told, let the legends rise,
The Ballard of Fiona, the princess in disguise.
